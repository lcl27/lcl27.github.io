About Cairn Financial Advisers LLP

Our Story

Cairn Financial Advisers LLP was established in 2009 by a team of experienced corporate finance professionals. Since our inception, we have built a reputation as one of the leading independent corporate finance advisory firms in London, specializing in IPOs, AIM, AQSE, and the Standard Listing segment of the London Stock Exchange.

Our commitment to providing expert, tailored advice has allowed us to successfully guide numerous companies through complex financial transactions and regulatory landscapes.

Our Expertise

  • IPO specialists for AIM, AQSE, and Standard Listing
  • AIM Nominated Adviser
  • AQSE Corporate Adviser
  • Takeover Code specialists
  • M&A advisory services
  • Regulatory compliance
