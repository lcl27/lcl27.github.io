Our Services

IPO Advisory

We guide companies through the complex process of Initial Public Offerings (IPOs) on AIM, AQSE, and the Standard Listing segment of the London Stock Exchange. Our comprehensive IPO services include:

  • Pre-IPO planning and structuring
  • Due diligence coordination
  • Prospectus preparation
  • Investor presentations and roadshows
  • Pricing and allocation strategies
  • Post-IPO support

AIM Nominated Adviser (Nomad) Services

As an approved Nomad, we provide expert guidance to companies listed or seeking to list on AIM. Our services include:

  • AIM Rules compliance advice
  • Corporate governance guidance
  • Ongoing regulatory support
  • Transaction vetting and approvals
  • Liaison with the London Stock Exchange

AQSE Corporate Adviser Services

For companies looking to list on the Aquis Stock Exchange (AQSE), we offer comprehensive advisory services:

  • AQSE admission process guidance
  • Regulatory compliance support
  • Corporate governance advice
  • Ongoing AQSE Rules interpretation and compliance

Takeover Code Expertise

Our team of Takeover Code specialists provides expert advice on all aspects of the City Code on Takeovers and Mergers, including:

  • Takeover defense strategies
  • Hostile bid management
  • Rule 9 waiver applications
  • Whitewash procedures
  • Concert party analysis

M&A Advisory

We provide comprehensive M&A advisory services for both public and private companies:

  • Buy-side and sell-side advisory
  • Deal structuring and negotiation
  • Valuation services
  • Due diligence coordination
  • Post-merger integration planning

Regulatory Compliance

Our team helps clients navigate the complex regulatory landscape of the UK financial markets:

  • FCA compliance advice
  • MAR compliance and training
  • Corporate governance best practices
  • Regulatory investigations support
